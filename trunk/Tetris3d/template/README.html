THE RULES OF THE GAME:

  1. As in simple tetris game, the goal is to fit the block into the net so that they occupy all the space on the bottom of the cuboid.

  2. As soon as any level is full, it will disappear, leaving space for new blocks.

  3. The game is over if the blocks reach the top of the cuboid.
